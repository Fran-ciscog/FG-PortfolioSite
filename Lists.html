Creating an "Ordered List" Project 1

An "Ordered list" of cooking instructions.
( Just follow these easy steps to Enjoy a delicious home-made Steak & Pasta dinner! )

  1. turn grill on high for 10 minutes
  2. add salt and pepper to Steaks
  3. boil water on high for 5 mins
  4. add salt to water
  5. add the Fettuccini pasta
  6. throw Steaks onto grill
  7. let pasta cook for 15 minutes
  8. boil Alfredo garlic sauce on medium for 8 minutes
  9. heat oven to 400°F
  10. heat bread in oven for 5 minutes
  11. flip steaks on grill
    cook both sides to medium rare
  12. chop up the Romain Lettuce
  13. chop up all the vegetables
  14. mix in all the vegetables and Romain Lettuce
    to make a salad
  15. add salad dressing
  16. Pour yourself a glass of Wine and
    Bon Apetite!
